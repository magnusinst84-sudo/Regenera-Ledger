# ESG ANNUAL REPORT 2024 (DRAFT)
## TechCorp Industries Inc.

**Overview:**
TechCorp Industries is committed to reducing its carbon footprint by 40% by 2030. Our primary emissions come from manufacturing plants in Ludhiana and logistics operations.

**Emissions Profile (2023):**
- Scope 1: 4,500 tCO2e (Direct combustion)
- Scope 2: 2,800 tCO2e (Purchased electricity)
- Scope 3: 12,400 tCO2e (Supply chain & logistics)

**Carbon Gap:**
Despite internal reductions, we have an unavoidable gap of approximately 3,000 tCO2e that requires high-quality carbon offsets from verified agricultural projects.

**Sustainability Goals:**
- Implement TCFD reporting standards.
- transition to 100% renewable energy for assembly lines.
- Partner with local farmers for regenerative soil offsets.
