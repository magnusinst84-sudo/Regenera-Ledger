EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ACME Manufacturing is committed to transparent environmental reporting in line
with GRI Standards and the TCFD framework. This report covers our direct (Scope 1),
indirect energy (Scope 2), and supply-chain (Scope 3) greenhouse gas emissions for
the financial year ending 31 December 2023.

GREENHOUSE GAS EMISSIONS SUMMARY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Total Scope 1 emissions:  1,200 tCO2e
Total Scope 2 emissions:  3,800 tCO2e
Total emissions: 5,000 tCO2e

We have achieved a 12% reduction in direct emissions compared to our 2022 baseline
of 5,700 tCO2e, primarily through renewable energy procurement and fleet electrification.

SCOPE 3 — SUPPLY CHAIN
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

We procure materials from the following disclosed Tier 1 suppliers:

- Green Fields Co (Mumbai) — agricultural raw materials, road transport
- EcoTex Fabrics (Chennai) — textile inputs, truck transport
- AgroSupply Ltd (Punjab) — grain commodities, rail transport
- Northern Grains (Haryana) — secondary grain sourcing, rail transport
- AlphaGoods (Nashik) — processed goods, truck transport
- BetaExports (Surat) — manufactured components, road transport

Our supplier management programme requires all Tier 1 vendors to submit
annual carbon disclosures. We partner with SGS India for third-party auditing
of our top 6 suppliers, who collectively account for approximately 65% of
our sourced volume.

We acknowledge that subcontractor and Tier 2 logistics data remain incomplete.
We are working with our procurement team to expand Scope 3 Category 4
(upstream transportation) disclosures in the 2024 cycle.
